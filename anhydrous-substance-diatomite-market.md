<p><p>The anhydrous substance diatomite market consists of three primary types: natural, calcined, and flux-calcined. Natural diatomite is unprocessed, retaining its original properties for industrial applications like filtration. Calcined diatomite is subjected to high temperatures, enhancing its absorbency and purity for uses in food and pharmaceuticals. Flux-calcined diatomite undergoes a specific heating process that alters its structure, improving its performance as a filler and in insulation. Each type serves distinct industries based on their unique characteristics and processing methods.</p></p>
