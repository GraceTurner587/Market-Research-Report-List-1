<p><p>The automotive suspension leaf spring market consists of two main types: mono leaf springs and multi leaf springs. Mono leaf springs utilize a single, curved leaf to provide support and flexibility, offering a lightweight and simple design that helps reduce weight in vehicles. Multi leaf springs, on the other hand, consist of several stacked leaves, enhancing load-carrying capacity and stability. This type is commonly used in heavier vehicles, providing better durability and improved handling under various conditions.</p></p>
