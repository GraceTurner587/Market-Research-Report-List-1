<p><p>The Electromagnetic Surgical Navigation System market is categorized into two main types: Hybrid Navigation and Electromagnetic Navigation. Hybrid Navigation combines both optical and electromagnetic technologies to enhance precision and flexibility during surgical procedures, allowing for real-time tracking and improved visualization. In contrast, Electromagnetic Navigation utilizes magnetic fields to pinpoint instrument locations within the surgical environment, providing a non-invasive solution that reduces reliance on line-of-sight. Both types aim to improve surgical accuracy and patient outcomes by facilitating precise navigation.</p></p>
