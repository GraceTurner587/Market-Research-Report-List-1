<p><p>The polypropylene foams market comprises two main types: expanded polypropylene (EPP) foams and extruded polypropylene (XPP) foams. Expanded polypropylene foams are lightweight, durable, and offer excellent cushioning properties, making them ideal for packaging, automotive, and insulation applications. Extruded polypropylene foams, on the other hand, are produced through a continuous extrusion process, resulting in a denser structure that provides enhanced thermal insulation and soundproofing. Both types cater to diverse industries, driven by their versatility and performance characteristics.</p></p>
