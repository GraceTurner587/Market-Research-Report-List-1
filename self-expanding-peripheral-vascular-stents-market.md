Elgiloy stents, made from a cobalt-chromium alloy, offer enhanced strength and durability, making them suitable for higher-stress environments. Nitinol stents, composed of a nickel-titanium alloy, provide excellent flexibility and are temperature-responsive, allowing them to expand at body temperature. Both types improve blood flow and reduce complications in peripheral vascular interventions.</p></p>
