<p><p>The CMP polishing pad market is divided into two primary types: hard CMP pads and soft CMP pads. Hard CMP pads are designed for aggressive material removal and are typically used for polishing semiconductor substrates, providing high durability and performance. In contrast, soft CMP pads are engineered for finer polishing applications, offering better conformability to surfaces and minimizing defects. Each type serves distinct purposes in semiconductor manufacturing processes, facilitating efficient material processing and achieving desired surface finishes.</p></p>
