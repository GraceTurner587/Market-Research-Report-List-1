<p><strong>The Military Specification Resistor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Through Hole</li><li>Surface Mount</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The military specification resistor market is categorized into two primary types: through-hole and surface mount. Through-hole resistors are larger components designed for insertion into pre-drilled holes on a circuit board, offering robust connections suitable for high-reliability applications. In contrast, surface mount resistors are smaller, designed for direct mounting on the surface of printed circuit boards, enabling compact designs and automated assembly processes. Both types are crucial for meeting stringent military standards of performance and durability in electronic applications.</p></p>
