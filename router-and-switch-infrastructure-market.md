<p><p>Router and switch infrastructure refers to the essential networking devices that facilitate data routing and switching among various devices in a network. Routers connect different networks, directing data packets between them, while switches operate within a single network, managing data flow between devices to enhance communication efficiency. These technologies are critical for maintaining high-performance network environments, particularly as businesses increasingly rely on cloud computing, IoT, and big data analytics.</p><p>The Router and Switch Infrastructure Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 10.5% during the forecast period. Additionally, the increased adoption of 5G technology and advancements in network security are also contributing to market expansion. Emerging trends include the shift towards software-defined networking (SDN), enhanced automation features, and virtualization, which improve network management and scalability.
