<p><ul><li>Natural Mullite</li><li>Fused Mullite</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mullite market is primarily categorized into natural and fused mullite types. Natural mullite is derived from bauxite and is commonly used in refractory applications due to its thermal stability and low thermal expansion. Fused mullite, on the other hand, is produced through the fusion of alumina and silica, offering enhanced durability and resistance to thermal shock. Both types cater to various industries, including ceramics, metallurgy, and construction, with applications in high-temperature environments.</p></p>
