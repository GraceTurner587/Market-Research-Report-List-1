<p><p>The bottled water processing market comprises various equipment types essential for production. Blow molders create plastic bottles from pre-forms, using heat to shape them. Shrink wrappers package bottles for protection and branding. Together, these components streamline the bottling process, enhancing efficiency, hygiene, and market readiness of bottled water products.</p></p>
