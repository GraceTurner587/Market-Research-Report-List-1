<p><p>Surface Acoustic Wave (SAW) filters operate by utilizing acoustic waves traveling along the surface of piezoelectric materials. The market is primarily categorized into transversal filters and resonator filters. Transversal filters are designed to process signals across multiple paths, effectively filtering out specific frequencies. In contrast, resonator filters focus on specific frequency responses, offering high selectivity and performance for narrowband applications. Both types are integral in telecommunications, consumer electronics, and industrial applications, enhancing signal integrity and performance.</p></p>
