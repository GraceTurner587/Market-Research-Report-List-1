<p><p>The self-retaining retractors market is divided into two main types: single-use and reusable. Single-use retractors are designed for one-time use, offering convenience and reducing the risk of cross-contamination, making them popular in clinical settings. In contrast, reusable retractors are made from durable materials, allowing for sterilization and multiple uses, which can be more cost-effective over time. Both types are integral in providing hands-free tissue retraction during surgeries, enhancing visibility and accessibility for surgeons.</p></p>
