The market is segmented into several types: </p><p>1. sTMS (single-pulse TMS) delivers isolated pulses for precise brain mapping.</p><p>2. pTMS (paired-pulse TMS) assesses cortical inhibition and facilitation.</p><p>3. rTMS (repetitive TMS) uses patterned stimuli for therapeutic effects, particularly in depression.</p><p>4. nTMS (navigated TMS) combines imaging for accurate targeting, enhancing treatment precision. Each type serves distinct diagnostic and therapeutic purposes within the broader TMS market.</p></p>
