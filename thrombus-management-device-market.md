<p><p>The thrombus management device market encompasses various types aimed at treating blood clots. Neurovascular embolectomy devices target clots in the brain, while embolectomy balloon catheters are used to remove blockages by inflating a balloon to restore blood flow. Percutaneous thrombectomy devices allow for minimally invasive removal of clots via catheterization. Lastly, catheter-guided thrombolysis devices deliver clot-dissolving medications directly to the site, enhancing efficacy and minimizing systemic effects, ensuring effective management of thrombotic conditions.</p></p>
