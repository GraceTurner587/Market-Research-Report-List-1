<p><p>Medium Chain Triglycerides (MCT) Oil is derived from coconut and palm kernel oils, consisting of medium-chain fatty acids. The market is divided into two main types: C6-C8 and C10-C12. C6-C8 MCTs, primarily caprylic and capric acid, are rapidly absorbed and metabolized, making them popular for energy and weight management. C10-C12 MCTs contain capric and lauric acids, offering a slower absorption rate with various health benefits. Both segments cater to different consumer needs, including dietary supplements and food products.</p></p>
