<p><ul><li>Positive Expiratory Pressure Devices</li><li>Flutter Devices</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Airway Clearance Devices System market encompasses various types that aid in facilitating mucus clearance from the respiratory system. Positive Expiratory Pressure (PEP) devices utilize expiratory resistance to keep airways open, enhancing mucus expectoration. Flutter devices combine PEP with oscillation to vibrate airways, assisting in mucus removal. Other devices may include high-frequency chest wall oscillation systems and manual techniques, providing alternative approaches for airway clearance. Each type aims to improve respiratory function and overall patient health outcomes.</p></p>
