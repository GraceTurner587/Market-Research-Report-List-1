<p><p>The satellite transponder market is categorized into several frequency bands: C Band, Ku Band, Ka Band, and K Band. C Band is used for wide-area coverage and is resistant to rain fade. Ku Band offers higher bandwidth and is favored for broadcasting and VSAT services. Ka Band provides even greater capacity and speed, ideal for high-throughput satellite services, while K Band, although less commonly used, allows for compact, high-frequency communication, supporting advanced applications. Each band serves distinct communication needs.</p></p>
