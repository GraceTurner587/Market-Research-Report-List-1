<p><ul><li>Dichroic Filters</li><li>Neutral Density (ND) Filters</li><li>Band-pass Filters</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The spectral sensing filter market comprises several types, each serving distinct purposes. Dichroic filters selectively transmit specific wavelengths while reflecting others, making them ideal for applications like color separation. Neutral Density (ND) filters uniformly reduce light intensity across wavelengths, enabling better control of exposure in imaging systems. Band-pass filters allow a certain range of wavelengths to pass, crucial for isolating specific spectral signals. Other filters may include notch filters or optical filters used in various scientific and industrial applications.</p></p>
