<p><p>Laser cutting machines for metals are categorized primarily into CO2, fiber, and YAG types. CO2 laser cutting machines are ideal for thick materials, offering high-quality cuts in non-metallic and some metallic surfaces. Fiber laser cutting machines excel in speed and precision, making them suitable for thin to medium metals, especially highly reflective materials. YAG cutting machines utilize neodymium-doped yttrium aluminum garnet lasers, providing excellent energy efficiency and versatility for various industrial applications, particularly in precision work and engraving.</p></p>
