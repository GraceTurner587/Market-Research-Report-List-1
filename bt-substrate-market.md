<p><strong>The BT Substrate Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>WB BGA</li><li>WB CSP</li><li>FC CSP</li></ul></p>
<p><p>BT substrate market types are categorized primarily into WB BGA (Wire Bond Ball Grid Array), WB CSP (Wire Bond Chip Scale Package), and FC CSP (Flip Chip Chip Scale Package). WB BGA is known for its robustness in high-performance applications, utilizing wire bonding for interconnections. WB CSP offers compact designs suitable for space-constrained environments, also using wire bonding. FC CSP, on the other hand, employs flip chip technology for superior electrical performance and thermal management, making it ideal for advanced semiconductor applications.</p></p>
