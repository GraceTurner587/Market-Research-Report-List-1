<p><p>Simultaneous Thermal Analyzer (STA) is an advanced analytical tool that measures the thermal properties of materials by combining both thermogravimetric analysis (TGA) and differential scanning calorimetry (DSC). STA is widely employed in industries such as pharmaceuticals, polymers, ceramics, and metallurgy for applications including material characterization, quality control, and formulation development.</p><p>The STA market is experiencing significant growth, driven by the increasing demand for quality analysis in R&D and quality assurance across various sectors.
