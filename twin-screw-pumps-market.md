<p><p>The twin-screw pump market comprises two main types: sealed and non-sealed twin-screw pumps. Sealed twin-screw pumps feature a hermetic design that prevents fluid leakage, making them ideal for handling hazardous or valuable liquids while ensuring environmental safety. In contrast, non-sealed twin-screw pumps are designed for applications where leakage is not a concern. They are typically simpler and more cost-effective, suitable for handling less critical fluids. Both types are widely used in industries like oil and gas, food processing, and chemicals.</p></p>
