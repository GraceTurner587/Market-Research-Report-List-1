Gum Arabic, a plant-derived substance, is prized for its ability to improve solubility and stabilize flavors. Other natural emulsifiers encompass sources like beeswax and vegetable gums, catering to diverse food formulations and meeting the demand for clean-label products in the industry.</p></p>
