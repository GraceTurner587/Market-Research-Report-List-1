<p><p>The disposable infusion sets market is categorized into two main types: pump infusion sets and non-pump infusion sets. Pump infusion sets are designed to work with infusion pumps, providing precise and controlled delivery of medications. They often incorporate features that enhance flow consistency and minimize occlusions. Non-pump infusion sets, on the other hand, are used for gravity or manual drip methods. These sets are typically simpler in design and are suited for various therapeutic applications, depending on patient needs and treatment protocols.</p></p>
