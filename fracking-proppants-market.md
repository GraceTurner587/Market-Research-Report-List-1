<p><p>Fracking proppants are essential materials used in hydraulic fracturing to keep fractures open, allowing hydrocarbons to flow more freely. The market is categorized into three types based on density: low density, medium density, and high density. Low-density proppants, such as bauxite and resin-coated sand, are lightweight and enhance flow-back efficiency. Medium-density proppants offer a balance between strength and weight, while high-density proppants, often ceramic materials, provide superior strength and durability, supporting greater pressures in deeper wells. Each type serves specific operational needs and conditions.</p></p>
