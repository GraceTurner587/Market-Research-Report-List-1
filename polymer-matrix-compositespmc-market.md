<p><p>Polymer Matrix Composites (PMCs) are materials made by combining a polymer matrix with reinforcement fibers, enhancing strength and durability. The market is primarily divided into two types: thermosets and thermoplastics. Thermosets, such as epoxy resins, offer excellent mechanical properties and thermal stability but are rigid and non-recyclable. In contrast, thermoplastics, like polycarbonate and nylon, are malleable and can be reshaped upon heating, making them recyclable and easier to process, catering to diverse applications across industries.</p></p>
