<p><strong>The Electronic Compact Fan Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Centrifugal Fan</li><li>Axial Fan</li><li>Mixed Flow Fan</li><li>Cross Flow Fan</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electronic compact fan market is categorized into four main types: </p><p>1. **Centrifugal Fans** utilize a rotating impeller to move air radially, providing high pressure and flow, ideal for ventilation systems. </p><p>2. **Axial Fans** move air parallel to the axis, offering effective cooling for electronic devices. </p><p>3. **Mixed Flow Fans** combine features of both centrifugal and axial fans, delivering versatile airflow. </p><p>4. **Cross Flow Fans** generate air movement across a wide area, making them suitable for compact spaces, enhancing thermal management in various applications.</p></p>
