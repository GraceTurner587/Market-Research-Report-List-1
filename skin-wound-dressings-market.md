<p><strong>The Skin Wound Dressings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Passive Dressing</li><li>Bioactive Dressing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The skin wound dressings market is primarily categorized into passive and bioactive dressings. Passive dressings serve as protective barriers and absorb exudate, encouraging a moist healing environment without therapeutic properties. In contrast, bioactive dressings contain active compounds that promote healing and tissue regeneration, often incorporating growth factors or antimicrobial agents. Both types address various wound types, with passive dressings being suitable for superficial wounds and bioactive dressings targeted at more complex or chronic wounds for enhanced healing outcomes.</p></p>
