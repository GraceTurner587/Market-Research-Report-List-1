<p><p>The conventional fire alarm system market features two primary types based on initiating circuits: one initiating circuit and multi-initiating circuits. </p><p>A one initiating circuit system links a single device, like a pull station or smoke detector, directly to the control panel, ideal for smaller spaces. In contrast, multi-initiating circuits connect multiple detectors or devices, allowing for comprehensive monitoring in larger facilities. This type enhances safety by enabling simultaneous alerts from various points, offering greater flexibility and responsiveness in fire detection.</p></p>
