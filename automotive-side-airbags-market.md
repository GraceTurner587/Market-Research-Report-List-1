<p><strong>The Automotive Side Airbags Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Side Torso Airbags</li><li>Side Tubular or Curtain Airbags</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive side airbags market comprises primarily two types: side torso airbags and side tubular (or curtain) airbags. Side torso airbags are designed to protect the upper body during lateral collisions by inflating between the occupant and the door or intruding objects. In contrast, side tubular airbags, also known as curtain airbags, deploy from the vehicle's roof to provide additional protection for the head and neck area, covering side windows and preventing occupants from being ejected during rollovers or side impacts.</p></p>
