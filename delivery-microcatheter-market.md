<p><p>The Delivery Microcatheter market is categorized into Single-Lumen and Dual-Lumen microcatheters. Single-Lumen microcatheters feature a single channel for fluid delivery, making them ideal for simple procedures where only one access point is needed. In contrast, Dual-Lumen microcatheters offer two separate channels, allowing for simultaneous delivery of multiple substances or fluids, enhancing efficiency in complex interventions. This versatility in design caters to a wide range of clinical applications, improving patient outcomes and procedural effectiveness in minimally invasive techniques.</p></p>
