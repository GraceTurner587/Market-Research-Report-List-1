<p><p>The traditional probe cards market is primarily categorized into Blade/Tungsten probe cards and Epoxy/Cantilever probe cards. Blade/Tungsten probe cards utilize rigid blades with tungsten tips for high precision and durability, ideal for demanding applications. In contrast, Epoxy/Cantilever probe cards feature flexible, epoxy-based designs that allow for better adaptability and cost-effectiveness in less stringent environments. Both types cater to different semiconductor testing needs, balancing performance, material costs, and probing requirements in the evolving electronics industry.</p></p>
