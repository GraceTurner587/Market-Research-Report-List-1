<p><p>The Transport Management System (TMS) market is segmented into several key types. Transport sourcing involves selecting the best carriers and services for shipments. Capacity management focuses on optimizing available transport resources. Performance management assesses the effectiveness of transport operations. Network design aims to create efficient distribution routes and facilities. Shipping consolidation combines multiple shipments to reduce costs and improve efficiency. Lastly, route planning utilizes algorithms to determine the most effective paths for deliveries, enhancing overall transport efficiency.</p></p>
