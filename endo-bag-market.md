<p><p>The Endo Bag market is categorized into two main types: detachable and non-detachable bags. Detachable endo bags feature a mechanism allowing them to be easily removed from the instruments after use, facilitating straightforward disposal and minimizing contamination risk. In contrast, non-detachable endo bags are designed to remain attached to the instruments throughout the procedure, providing a more integrated approach during surgeries. Both types serve vital roles in various surgical procedures, enhancing efficiency and safety in minimally invasive techniques.</p></p>
