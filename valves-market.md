<p><p>Valves are critical components in fluid control systems, categorized into several types based on their function. Gate valves are used for on/off control, allowing minimal flow restriction when fully open. Control valves regulate flow and pressure, adapting to varying process conditions. Ball valves provide quick shutoff with a spherical disc, ensuring tight sealing. Butterfly valves use a rotating disc to manage flow with compact design. Other valves include check, globe, and solenoid types, each serving specific applications in industrial and commercial settings.</p></p>
