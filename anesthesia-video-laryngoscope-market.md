<p><p>The Anesthesia Video Laryngoscope market comprises two main types: channelled and un-channelled video laryngoscopes. Channelled video laryngoscopes feature a built-in channel that allows for the passage of various instruments, enhancing the ease of intubation and improving visual access. In contrast, un-channelled video laryngoscopes lack this channel, focusing solely on providing visual aid during the intubation process. Both types are essential tools in anesthesia, improving patient safety and success rates in difficult intubation scenarios.</p></p>
