<p><p>The piezoelectric ceramic wafer market primarily consists of three main types: Lead Zinc Titanates (PZT), Lead Titanate, and Lead Magnesium Niobate. PZT is widely used for its high sensitivity and broad frequency range, making it ideal for sensors and actuators. Lead Titanate exhibits strong piezoelectric properties and is often used in high-performance applications. Lead Magnesium Niobate offers enhanced thermal stability and lower dielectric losses, making it suitable for resonators and transducers. Each material serves specific applications based on performance characteristics.</p></p>
